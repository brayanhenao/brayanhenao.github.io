About me

My name is Brayan, I'm 21 years old. I'm studying Software Systems Engineering (currently IX semester) at Icesi University. I like to listen to music, watch series, play video games and coding in my free times or in vacation.


I consider myself as a responsible person. I like to travel a lot when I can, but mostly I travel in car with my family, I enjoy that kind of transport because you can discover so many places that you didn't know yet. Normally I travel with comfort clothes such as trousers and t-shirts. In my travels I can sleep in almost every sleeping quarters, I have slept in the ground, comfortable beds and hammocks. Food is a hard thing for me when I travel, I'm a little bit picky with it, especially with seafood.
